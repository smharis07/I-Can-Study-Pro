### Getting Started: Your First Subject
When you first open the app, you'll see a friendly welcome screen. The best way to start is by
creating your first subject.
1. Click the big **"Create Your First Subject"** button.
2. A new "Subject Card" will appear. Simply click on the title ("New Subject") and type in the

3. That's it! You've planted your first seed.

### The Main Workspace: Building Your Hive
Your workspace is built on a simple three-level structure:
* **Subjects:** The main category (e.g., Math).
* **Chapters:** Sections within a subject (e.g., Algebra).
* **Topics:** The specific things you're studying (e.g., Quadratic Equation).
You can **add**, **rename**, **delete**, and **reorder** any of these cards using the buttons on
each card header and the little grip handle to drag and drop.
Click the little arrow on any card to expand it and see what's inside or collapse it to keep your
workspace tidy.
### Mastering a Topic: The Real Magic Happens Here
When you expand a "Topic" card, you'll find the core tools to help you learn.
#### 1. Cookie Analysis (How Do You *Really* Feel?)
This is your self-assessment tool. For each topic, you rate your confidence across four areas.
Be honest! This is how the app learns where you need the most help. Steps: (how to utilize it for
performance).
1. Time spent studying: How much effort have you put in?
2. Answering curveball questions: Can you apply the knowledge in; practice questions or
tricky ways?
3. Confidence recalling facts: Can you remember the facts while practicing?
4. Time spent relearning: How often do you have to go back and review this?
Your selections here calculate the topic's **Score %**.

### Keeping Your Data Safe
Your data is stored locally in your browser. To protect it, use these tools:
* **Backup: This downloads a JSON file of all your data to your computer. Do this regularly!
* **Restore (Replace): This will **completely delete** your current data and replace it with the
data from a backup file.
* **Restore (Merge): This intelligently combines a backup file with your current data, adding
new things without deleting what you already have. This is useful if you use the app on two
different computers.
### âš ï¸ Words of Caution: Handle With Care!
To make sure everything runs smoothly, please keep these things in mind:
1. This App is Local: All your hard work is saved in **your web browser's storage** on this
specific computer. It's not in the cloud.
CRITICAL: If you "Clear Browsing Data" or "Clear Site Data" for your browser, **you will
lose everything.
2. Backup is Your Best Friend: Please, please, *please* use the **Backup** button regularly!
Save the file somewhere safe like Google Drive or Device Storage. If your computer crashes,
this backup file is the only way to get your data back.
3. Know Your Restore Options: Understand the difference between **Restore (Replace)** and
**Restore (Merge)**. Accidentally using "Replace" when you meant to "Merge" can cause you to
lose recent work.
4. Be Honest with the Cookies: The "Weak Topics" and "Focus Session" features are only as
good as your self-assessment. Be honest about your confidence levels to get the most accurate
guidance.
